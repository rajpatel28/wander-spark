Ottawa

Top things to do and experience

Downtown & Parliament Hill

ByWard Market

Highlights: Historic open-air market with artisan stalls, fresh produce, local boutiques, cafés, and Ottawa’s iconic BeaverTails.

Cost: Free (food and shopping extra)

Tips: Visit in the morning for quieter browsing; evenings get lively with patios and street performers.

Ottawa Locks

Highlights: Historic hand-operated locks connecting the Rideau Canal to the Ottawa River; scenic viewpoints and perfect photo spots.

Cost: Free

Tips: Walk the pathway beside the locks for great angles of the Chateau Laurier and Parliament Hill.

Parliament Hill

Highlights: Canada’s most iconic landmark with Gothic Revival architecture, sweeping river views, and historic grounds.

Cost: Free

Tips: Free tours to the Parliament can be booked in advance. Early mornings offer the best lighting and minimal crowds for photos.

Peace Tower

Highlights: Iconic Parliament bell tower, memorial chamber, and panoramic views from the observation area.

Cost: Free

Tips: Go right at opening — security lines get long later in the day.

National War Memorial

Highlights: Iconic granite arch, Tomb of the Unknown Soldier, and ceremonial Changing of the Guard during summer months.

Cost: Free

Tips: Visit at sunset for dramatic lighting; mornings are calm if you want quiet photos.

Confederation Park

Highlights: Peaceful urban park with memorial sculptures, fountains, tree-lined paths, and easy access to nearby landmarks.

Cost: Free

Tips: A great place for a mid-day break — shaded benches and close to ByWard Market for snacks.

Museums & Galleries

Canadian War Museum

Highlights: Award-winning architecture, tanks and military vehicles, interactive exhibits, and Canada’s full wartime history.

Cost: ~$20 CAD (Free on Thursdays, 5 pm – 8 pm)

Tips: Don’t skip the LeBreton Gallery — it’s where the massive tanks and artillery are displayed.

National Gallery of Canada

Highlights: World-class art museum, glass cathedral-style architecture, Indigenous art collections, and the iconic “Maman” spider sculpture.

Cost: ~$20 CAD (Free on Thursdays, 5 pm – 8 pm)

Tips: Head up to the Great Hall — best panoramic views of Parliament and the river.

Canadian Museum of History

Highlights: Incredible Grand Hall with towering totem poles, detailed First Peoples exhibits, and an IMAX theatre.

Cost: ~$22 CAD (Free on Thursdays, 5 pm – 8 pm)

Tips: Cross the river viewpoint outside — amazing skyline shots of Parliament from Gatineau.

Bank of Canada Museum

Highlights: History of Canadian money, interactive finance exhibits, and a surprisingly fun look at currency and economics.

Cost: Free

Tips: Perfect rainy-day option — it’s quick, central, and rarely crowded.

Canada Aviation & Space Museum

Highlights: Massive aircraft collection, aviation tech, cockpits, and Canada’s space exploration exhibits.

Cost: ~$18 CAD (Free on Thursdays, 5 pm – 8 pm)

Tips: The Cold War jet section and helicopter lineup are fan favorites — give extra time if you like engineering.

Landmarks & Scenic Spots

Rideau Canal

Highlights: UNESCO World Heritage Site, scenic walking and biking paths, iconic views of Parliament and Chateau Laurier.

Cost: Free

Tips: The canal-side trail between the locks and Dow’s Lake is the most picturesque stretch.

Rideau Falls

Highlights: Twin waterfalls flowing into the Ottawa River, scenic viewpoints, peaceful lookout spots.

Cost: Free

Tips: Best during spring and early summer; bring a camera for river views.

Dow’s Lake

Highlights: Lakeside boardwalk, gardens, canoe/pedal-boat rentals, peaceful waterfront vibes.

Cost: Free (rentals ~$25–40)

Tips: Go around sunset — the golden hour reflections on the lake are gorgeous.

Rideau Hall

Highlights: Official residence of the Governor General, ceremonial guards, beautifully landscaped grounds.

Cost: Free

Tips: Check if the ceremonial guard is scheduled — great photos and minimal crowds.

Notre-Dame Cathedral

Highlights: Stunning twin-spire basilica, ornate interior, painted ceilings, and peaceful atmosphere.

Cost: Free

Tips: Interior photography is allowed — visit on a sunny day for the stained-glass lighting.

Tours & Experiences

Haunted Walk of Ottawa

Highlights: Ghost stories, prison tours, nighttime walking tour.

Cost: ~$27 CAD

Aquabus on Ottawa River

Highlights: Scenic cruise with views of Parliament, bridges, and Gatineau; relaxing and perfect for sunset.

Cost: ~$30 CAD

Tips: Choose the sunset departure — the light on Parliament is unreal.

Royal Canadian Mint

Highlights: See how collector coins are made, hold a real gold bar, guided tours through the facility.

Cost: ~$12 CAD

Tips: Tours are timed — book your slot early in the day.
