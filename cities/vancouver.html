Stanley Park

Seawall Walk / Bike

Highlights: Stunning waterfront views, Totem poles, Vancouver skyline.

Cost: Free to walk, $10-$15 for bike rental.

Tips: Go early to avoid crowds. Bring water and sunscreen.

Cost-effective alternative: Rent bikes from nearby local shops for cheaper rates.

Vancouver Aquarium

Highlights: Marine life exhibits, touch pools, interactive displays.

Cost: $40 CAD

Tips: Arrive before feeding times for the shows.

Cost-effective alternative: Check city passes for discounted tickets.

Granville Island

Public Market

Highlights: Fresh produce, artisan food, local crafts.

Cost: Free entry, food varies.

Tips: Mid-morning for fewer crowds.

Cost-effective alternative: Buy small snacks instead of sit-down meals.

Granville Island Brewery

Highlights: Sample local craft beers, brewery tour.

Cost: Free tasting, tours $10 CAD.

Tips: Book tour in advance.

Cost-effective alternative: Skip the tour and just taste the beers at the bar.

Grouse Mountain

Skyride & Mountaintop Views

Highlights: Scenic gondola ride, panoramic views, wildlife refuge.

Cost: $65 CAD

Tips: Visit in the morning for clear skies.

Cost-effective alternative: Hike the Grouse Grind for free (very strenuous).

Zipline Adventure

Highlights: Adrenaline rush, stunning forest views.

Cost: $80 CAD

Tips: Book online for best rates.

Cost-effective alternative: Skip zipline if on a budget; enjoy mountaintop walks.

Gastown & Downtown

Gastown Steam Clock & Walking Tour

Highlights: Historic area, cobblestone streets, shops and cafes.

Cost: Free

Tips: Walk in daylight to catch the Steam Clock in action.

Cost-effective alternative: Self-guided walking tour is free; skip guided tours.

Canada Place & Waterfront Walk

Highlights: Waterfront views, iconic sails, cruise ships.

Cost: Free

Tips: Evening walk for sunset photography.

Cost-effective alternative: Enjoy views and photos for free; skip paid attractions nearby.

Kitsilano & Beaches

Kitsilano Beach

Highlights: Sandy beach, volleyball courts, ocean views.

Cost: Free

Tips: Go early for parking and space.

Cost-effective alternative: Bring own snacks and drinks.

Vanier Park & Museum of Vancouver

Highlights: Museums, scenic park views, cultural exhibits.

Cost: $20 CAD (Museum)

Tips: Check for free museum days.

Cost-effective alternative: Walk the park and enjoy scenery for free.

Capilano & North Vancouver

Capilano Suspension Bridge

Highlights: Iconic suspension bridge, rainforest, treetop adventure.

Cost: $60 CAD

Tips: Go early to avoid crowds.

Cost-effective alternative: Explore nearby Capilano River trails for free.

Lynn Canyon Park & Suspension Bridge

Highlights: Hiking trails, suspension bridge, waterfalls.

Cost: Free

Tips: Great alternative to Capilano for smaller crowds.

Cost-effective alternative: Just explore the trails; no entrance fee.
